❓ FAQ

How long does token crediting take?
Most top-ups are processed within 24 hours during business days. Once processed, your tokens are immediately available — no restart needed.
Do tokens expire?
No! Purchased tokens never expire. They remain in your account until you use them.
Can I get a refund?
We offer a 7-day money-back guarantee on unused tokens. Contact us at hello@kopiaiagent.com with your transaction ID.
How do I find my API key prefix?
Your API key prefix is the first part of your key before the random characters (e.g., kopi-xxxx...). You can find it in your ~/.kopi/.env file or wherever you saved your key.
